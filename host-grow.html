Host a GROW Conference

Deadline: January 31, year n-1
(To host GROW in year n, please contact the steering committee by January 31 one year prior)

How to host a GROW conference

Please let the steering committee know if you are interested in hosting a GROW conference, preferably including the following information:

1. Host Organization name

2. Information on Workshop Facilities (e.g., room capacity, equipment, accessibility)

3. Tentative organizing committee

4. Preferred year(s) you wish to host GROW

. Financing plan (the organising committee is responsible for securing funding)

Email the steering committee grow-europe@.... The steering committee will review proposals and get back to you within 6 weeks of the submission deadline.
The conference:
  • Runs annually on typically early April
  • Is primarily open to bachelor students from EU, EEA, EFTA, UK, and the Schengen Area, including international students. Early masters students are also encouraged to apply.

Hosts are typically departments or groups of departments offering Ph.D. programs in the mathematical sciences. We help the organising committees to prepare proposals, however they are responsible for securing all necessary funding.

Typical budget

The conference is usually budgeted around €20,000-25,000. In 2023, the total cost of hosting GROW was approximately €16,000. Typical expenses include: travel and three nights accommodation for 50 individuals including participants, speakers and panelists. Refreshments, two lunches, and a banquet. Participants are typically hosted in shared rooms to minimize accommodation costs.

Typical timeline for GROW on year n

This may vary based on local academic calendars, host organisations' internal calendars, and other factors, but generally includes:
  • confirm speaker line-up by mid-october year n-1, with posters and website going live (most of which can be prepared ahead of time)
  • advertising starts as soon as speakers are confirmed
  • late December year n-1: local volunteers (PhD, PD, faculty members) notified.
  • early January year n: application portal opens
  • Mid February deadline for registration with financial support
  • Late February: final participant list confirmed
  • Early April: GROW conference takes place
